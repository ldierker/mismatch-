mismatch
mismatch was created to make fashion more accessible to those with visual impairments, specifically focusing on color blindness. Our application provides a detailed description of the colors of your clothing choices and an analysis of how these colors interact. With this information, you can give your outfit a second look with the click of a button.

To use mismatch, upload up to 3 images of your clothing choices for a given outfit to our color analysis page. For the best results, the majority of your photo should contain the article of clothing to be analyzed and should be placed against a solid background in good lighting.

Good luck matching!
